About Us

Inside Out Apparel



Chairwoman & Founder

Inside Out apparel is a high-quality custom-made unisex reversible jacket and sweatshirt. Ms. Edriana A. Fernandez, the chairwoman of Inside Out apparel also come up with the idea of providing a custom-made jacket and sweatshirt, especially for customers who are interested in a clothing line. She aims to promote a new variety of jacket and sweatshirt that will be recognized as an unique apparel brand around the world.



The Company

In the year 2021, Ms. Fernandez was a reseller of clothes that she bought at tiange in Taytay, Rizal. The clothes he usually buys and sells are dusters for adults, ternos for children, and shorts for any customers. As a teenager with experience in sales and knowledge about clothes. She considered opening her apparel store that sells jackets and sweatshirts because they are popular among Filipinos, particularly young people. Filipinos wear jackets and sweatshirts regardless of whether in the Philippines. In the year 2023, Inside Out Apparel was launched. The first store was located at 2600 Legarda St. Sampaloc, Manila. Ms. Fernandez starts promoting Inside Out Apparel with the help of well-known influencers by sending them the products and asking them to post them on their social media. Place it as an advertisement to make them more recognizable and work congruently to achieve the goals.



Product

A reversible jacket is a kind of jacket that can be worn two ways, it gives a fashionable appearance. Inside Out Apparel offers a reversible piece of clothing inside out instantly doubles the number of looks, you get two pieces for the price of one. It’s a great way to make your wardrobe go so much further. When it comes to saving suitcase or closet space, a reversible piece of clothing makes a lot of sense. Our stylish yet casual Reversible Jackets are lightly quilted for warmth, so easy to pull on as an extra layer. Overall the jackets are also thicker, as they are made up of two layers sewn together. In the case of our reversible jacket these are cotton layers with wadding in-between.


Packaging

Inside out apparel believes that a good packaging will make a product stand out from the competition. That is why we produced a trendy tote bag for our trendy products. Also we want to provide a packaging that is durable and recyclable, and economically viable. Our company produced a tote bag out of the excess cottons from the jacket. Furthermore, it can be use while shopping around town, and also they can reuse it to transports foods, store personal care, and household items. Whether for fashion or functionality, the tote bag is the convenient bag for any purpose.
